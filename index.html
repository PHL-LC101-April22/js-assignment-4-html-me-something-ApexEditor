Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z: A story about Son Goku, a Saiyan from planet Vegeta that crash landed on Earth as an infant who grows to become earths strongest hero destined to defend the planet against the mightiest villains in the galaxy. With the help of his Earthly friends, Goku ventures the Earth in quest to find the mystical seven dragon balls. When all seven of the dragon balls are collected they will grant you any one wish you want! The creator of the Anime is the creative genius, Akira Toriyama

Spirited Away

Spirited Away: Japans highest grossing film in history due to its stunning visuals of magical creatures and revolutionary animation, this story is a fantasy adventure focused on an adolescent girl named Chihiro who later is renamed Sen. Young Chihiro and her parents stumble across the threshold into a spirited world that tests the young girls maturity and character. She must summon her courage to withstand the chaos of this new world in order to reverse the curse her parents endured for entering the foreign world and set them free. The great Hayao Miyazaki is the director.

Vampire Hunter D

The setting is a dark-fantasy post apocalyptic world plagued by many different breeds of monsters. The story is about a vampire hunter, named D, who is also a member of the dark world of bloodlusted beings known as vampires. Tasked by protecting a young girl who was bitten by the vampire lord of the land, Count Magnus Lee, the hero D must slay the Counts minions and end the Vampire lords reign. This story was created by Hideyuki Kikuchi.

Yu Yu Hakusho

A high school troubled teen, Yusuke Urameshis' life is drastically changed when he does a good deed to save a life which cost his own life in the process. The ruler of the afterlife restores Yusukes life as he’s granted the role of spirit detective, a mediator between the human realm and the demon realm. Through his journey, Yusuke gains friends as he grows stronger while struggling to control his new found abilities. The creator of this manga(Japanese comic book) turned anime is Yoshishiro Togashi.

Hunter X Hunter

The Journey of a pragmatic youth, Gon who becomes a world respected hunter which is a licensed professional that specialize in hunting anyone and anything. While gaining special abilities, Gon travels through the world to reconnect with his father who is a hunter too. The creator of Hunter x Hunter is Yoshishiro Togashi.

Ghost in the Shell

A Sci-fi tech thriller In the very distant future where androids and humans live amongst one another. The Major, a cyborg-human hybrid special operations agent proceeds to take down a hacker who manipulates the brains of androids to control them to carry out his will. The original creator of Ghost in the shell is Masamune Shirow.

Death Note

A Psychological mystery-thriller set in 21st century Japan. The story is set around a genius young man named Light Yagami who stumbled across a powerful book not of this world called the death note. These books are normally wielded by Shinigami(Soul reapers). When in the hands of a human, this book grants its user the power of God by deciding who, when and how another person meets their demise. Light takes on a duel personality as almighty judge of the world known as Kira as well as being Light, who is taking on the task of leading the task force to take down Kira for the sake of humanity. The co-creators of Death Note are Takeshi Obata and Tsugumi Ohba

Honorable Mentions:

Cowboy Bebop

Space bounty hunters Jet Black and Spike Spiegel journey throughout the known galaxy chasing down universal criminals in order to make ends meet. Jet balances his troubled past along side cleaning up what ever mess Spike seems to drum up. Spike is constantly haunted by his chaotic past while all aboard this trusty space vessel named Bebop. The pair is often accompanied by a beautiful thief with zero knowledge of her past due to being cryogenically frozen for decades. The creator is the EVER so cool Shinichiro Watanabe

Samurai Champloo

This story is a fusion hip-hop and Samurai action drama RomCom during an undetermined time in Japans Edo period. A woman named Fuu is attacked by local thugs and is ultimately saved by 2 of the very uncommon combination of people, an obnoxious and unpredictable swordsman named Mugen and a quiet Ronin(a samurai who has no master) named Jin. Wanted in connection of accidentally killing a politicians son, Jin and Mugen are aided in escaping prosecution with the help of young Fuu. The trio set off to help Fuu find a mysterious Samurai that Smells of sunflowers and along the journey they get to know one another.The creator is...thats right you guessed it once again Shinichiro Watanabe
